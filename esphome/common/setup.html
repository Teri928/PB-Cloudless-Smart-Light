Bulb Setup Opions

Welcome! Use this page to perform the initial setup for your smart bulb. Follow the steps below:

  1. Enter a friendly name for your bulb.
  2. Optionally, set a static IP if you want to fix it on your network.
  3. Click Sync Time & Name to update the bulb’s clock and save your settings.
  4. After this, SNTP will automatically keep your bulb’s time accurate.
